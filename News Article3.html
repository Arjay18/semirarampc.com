TREE PLANTING ACTIVITY

Published on January 1, 2024 by Author Name

On November 10, 2024, the Semirara Multi-purpose Cooperative (SEMCO) organized a significant tree-planting event, planting 250 Kalamansi plants in front of the SMPC Swimming pool.


This activity aimed to enrich the environment while fostering unity and responsibility among members. It raised awareness about environmental conservation and encouraged participation in eco-friendly initiatives. Participants enjoyed the experience, reinforcing the importance of environmental stewardship and community engagement.
